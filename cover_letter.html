My Cover Letter

Santiago Noacco

I am an experienced Data Scientist with technical experience in image recognition, time series analysis and prediction, clustering methods and an extensive experimentation background that I will summarize below. In addition I have led and developed many projects from scratch to production, and negotiated the planning terms, features and deliverables with high hierarchy stakeholders. The most rewarding task however, was to help junior colleagues grow in their role.

All my projects have been and always will be driven by value first, and that is the north I make decisions upon. I have also developed a very fast, efficient and high quality workflow that facilitates the development of profitable and scalable products.

I got a Master in experimental physics at the university of La Plata (Argentina) with a study using Deep Neural Networks (CNN architecture) to improve the efficiency in photon identification for the ATLAS collaboration. Currently I am under a PhD career following the same studies and I have been working as a (Sr.) Data Scientist for a mobile game company in the Publishing & User Acquisition data department. In that role I helped grow the revenue, and the volume of users by significant margins in a very fast fashion; without compromising quality, clarity & scalability of our products. Finally I was a key agent in decision making and project planning strategies and execution.

In the future I would like to further develop my technical leading skills to be able to build great teams that provide awesome products.
